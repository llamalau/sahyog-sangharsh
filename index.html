WhatsApp-Based Special Education Platform

A dual-interface system connecting Special Educators and caregivers. SEs plan and assign activities through a dashboard; caregivers receive tasks, watch tutorials, and submit evidence through a mobile app — all orchestrated via WhatsApp and the NIEPID Disha curriculum.
